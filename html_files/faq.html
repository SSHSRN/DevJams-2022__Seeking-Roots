Frequently Asked Questions
Q: What is this website all about?
A: Seeking Roots is a web-application where users can register themselves providing their basic details and write posts or stories about themselves (AutoBiographies) and also about their near and dear ones (Biographies).
Q: What are its features?
A: It allows to add posts about themselves or somebody they know. Allows to upload multimedia files and store it securely in the cloud(FireBase Storage). It can also generate a downloadable PDF file for the article written.
Q: What all technologies it uses?
A: For frontend it uses HTML5, CSS3 and javascript while for backend it uses firebase.
Q: Why should I use it?
A: It is a reliable web application which our testimonials also prove.
